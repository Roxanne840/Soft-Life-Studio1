## 🚀 Universal Marketing Stack

Regardless of which product you choose, these platforms and strategies work for all digital products:

### Platforms to Sell On
- **Gumroad** — best for creators, instant setup
- **Etsy** — huge built-in audience for templates and printables
- **Payhip** — no transaction fees on free plan
- **Teachable / Kajabi** — best for courses and memberships
- **Amazon KDP** — great for ebooks

### Traffic Sources (Free)
- **Pinterest** — evergreen traffic, best for visual products
- **TikTok** — fastest organic growth in 2026
- **YouTube Shorts** — builds trust and authority
- **Instagram Reels** — great for lifestyle and wellness niches
- **Email List** — your most valuable long-term asset

### Paid Ads (When Ready)
- **Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Ads** — best for targeting women 25–45
- **Pinterest Ads** — high buying intent audience
- **Google Ads** — great for template and tool searches

## 💡 Key Success Principles

1. **Solve a specific problem** — niche products outsell generic ones every time
2. **Start with one product** — master selling it before adding more
3. **Build your email list from day one** — it is your most valuable asset
4. **Price for value, not time** — digital products scale because you make them once
5. **Reinvest early profits into ads** — even $5/day on Meta can compound fast
6. **Use testimonials everywhere** — social proof is the #1 conversion driver
7. **Bundle products** — bundles increase average order value by 3–5x
